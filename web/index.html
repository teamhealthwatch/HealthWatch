Welcome to HealthWatch!

Healthwatch project will track a user’s heart rate when requested and make decisions that may help the user including dialing stored emergency contacts by phone or text. HealthWatch will monitor a user’s heart rate at all times and when it determines a rate above a certain point that will be specified, users would be asked to verify their status. A user will need to say if they need help or if they are okay. If a user is nonresponsive, the app will call the user’s primary emergency contact and notify their other contacts via text with a google map location link. Users can also use HealthWatch to manually send their locations to emergency contacts, so their contacts can avoid worrying where the users are. HealthWatch can also help first responders by displaying a brief summary of a user’s health history. Another core feature will be our medication tracker, alerting the user when certain medication needs to be taken during the day. We will also store health data about the user and will be able to generate graphs to display their health history in specific a range of time.

Created By Faith Oladele, Ryan Flecter, and Yan Tan.
